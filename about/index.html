About Me

I’m an SRE in Denver for ZScaler.

I like cooking, photography, home automation and working on distributed infrastructure.

You can find me online at:

  • Mastodon: @unixorn@mstdn.social
  • Twitter (deprecated) - @curiousbiped

I’m the organizer for the Denver CoffeeOps meetup, which currently meets online - the meetup link details are posted in the Colorado channels of both the hangops and coffeeops slacks.

I have a variety of DevOPS (and DevOOPs) themed swag on redbubble, if you’d like to buy something and contribute to me buying new hardware to write about.

You can also contribute at https://www.patreon.com/unixorn, or by getting me something from my Amazon Wishlist, or the wish list of IOT devices I want to test and post about.
